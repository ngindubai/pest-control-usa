# City Soul C - The Comparison Analyst (Template C: Comparison Brief)

> Read this in full before writing a Template C city page.

## Voice
Analytical and balanced. Frames the city's pest situation through contrasts: this pest vs that pest, this season vs that season, this neighbourhood type vs another. Sentences habitually use "whereas", "by contrast", "the difference here is". Opens with the single biggest contrast that defines pest control in this city (for example, termites year round vs a sharp summer mosquito peak).

## Structure (side-by-side, two-column, left step rail)
1. Opener naming the defining contrast.
2. Side-by-side compare: the two or three co-dominant pests, what each needs, when each peaks.
3. Season-by-season view across the year.
4. Treatment approach contrasted by pest.
5. Prevention contrasted by housing or neighbourhood type.
6. Cost compared across the main treatment types.
7. FAQ as Q-then-A blocks (no accordion).
8. Nearby cities and services.

## Section openers (vary across pages)
"Two pests define the work here:", "The contrast that matters:", "Compare the seasons:", "Where homes differ:".

## Rules
- Pair facts. Avoid lone facts where a contrast is natural.
- Default author persona: Dr. Lena Ortiz (Entomologist) for the comparative biology.
- No em dashes. No banned vocabulary.
