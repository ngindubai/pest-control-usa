Pest Control in Texas, FAQs

Do you service all areas of Texas?

Yes. We serve all major cities and surrounding areas across Texas, including Houston, San Antonio, Dallas, Austin, Fort Worth. If you're outside a major metro, call us, we can typically dispatch a technician within 24 to 48 hours.

How quickly can you respond in Texas?

Same-day service is available in most Texas metro areas. For non-emergency situations, we typically schedule within 24 hours. For pest emergencies (stinging insects, wildlife, severe infestations), call our 24/7 line for immediate dispatch.

Are your Texas technicians licensed?

We work with licensed, insured local pest control technicians, and we match you with pros who meet Texas's pesticide applicator licensing requirements. Ask your assigned technician for their license and insurance details before work begins.
