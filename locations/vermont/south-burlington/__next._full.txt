Tick habitat in South Burlington is mainly the wooded residential edges: areas where the lawn meets tree lines, brush piles, unmaintained borders, and the Winooski River corridor. From a field standpoint, professional yard treatment targets the leaf litter zone and the lawn-to-woodland edge where ticks wait for a host.
