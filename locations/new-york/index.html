Do you service all areas of New York?

Yes. We serve all major cities and surrounding areas across New York, including New York City, Buffalo, Rochester, Syracuse, Albany. If you're outside a major metro, call us, we can typically dispatch a technician within 24 to 48 hours.

How quickly can you respond in New York?

Same-day service is available in most New York metro areas. For non-emergency situations, we typically schedule within 24 hours. For pest emergencies (stinging insects, wildlife, severe infestations), call our 24/7 line for immediate dispatch.

We work with licensed, insured local pest control technicians, and we match you with pros who meet New York's pesticide applicator licensing requirements. Ask your assigned technician for their license and insurance details before work begins.
