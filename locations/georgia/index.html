Pest Control in Georgia, FAQs

Do you service all areas of Georgia?

Yes. We serve all major cities and surrounding areas across Georgia, including Atlanta, Augusta, Columbus, Savannah, Athens. If you're outside a major metro, call us, we can typically dispatch a technician within 24 to 48 hours.

How quickly can you respond in Georgia?

Same-day service is available in most Georgia metro areas. For non-emergency situations, we typically schedule within 24 hours. For pest emergencies (stinging insects, wildlife, severe infestations), call our 24/7 line for immediate dispatch.

Are your Georgia technicians licensed?

We work with licensed, insured local pest control technicians, and we match you with pros who meet Georgia's pesticide applicator licensing requirements. Ask your assigned technician for their license and insurance details before work begins.

What are the most common pest problems in Georgia?

In Georgia, the most frequently treated pests are Termites, Fire Ants, Cockroaches, Mosquitoes, Fleas. Georgia's humid subtropical climate and red clay soils support massive subterranean termite colonies, Atlanta-area homes have among the highest documented termite damage rates in the Southeast.
