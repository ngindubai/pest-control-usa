Financing FAQs

Does applying affect my credit score?

No. We perform a soft credit inquiry only, which does not affect your credit score. A hard inquiry is only performed if you accept a financing offer.

What is the minimum treatment amount for financing?

Our 0% interest plan is available on services of $300 or more. Extended plans are available for $500+. Monthly subscription plans do not require financing.

How quickly am I approved?

Most applicants receive a decision in 2 to 5 minutes after submitting the short application. You can proceed with scheduling your service immediately upon approval.

Can I pay off my balance early?

Yes, absolutely. There are no prepayment penalties on any of our financing plans. Paying early saves you from any interest that would have accrued.

Is financing available for ongoing service plans?

Ongoing monthly and quarterly service plans are billed on a per-visit or monthly basis and do not require separate financing, they are already structured as low monthly payments.
